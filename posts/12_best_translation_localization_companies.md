## ⚙️ Key Criteria for Choosing a Translation Vendor

If you choose to outsource your localization project, selecting the right partner is critical. Use these criteria to ensure a smooth, high-quality partnership:

### 1. Check for Certifications

- **Mandatory:** When evaluating your new partner, look for **ISO 9001** (quality management), **ISO 17100** (translation quality), and **ISO 28001** (information security).
- **Relevance:** These certifications are particularly important for regulated industries (pharmaceuticals, finance) to ensure regulatory compliance and secure data handling.

### 2. Require Subject Matter Expertise (SME)

- **The Risk:** A generalist vendor may translate words accurately but miss the critical context and nuances of your industry.
- **The Solution:** It is crucial to work with a translation company that has **specific expertise in your niche** (e.g., life sciences, aerospace, financial services). Without a subject matter expert, a technical phrase can be misinterpreted, leading to significant errors.

### 3. Ask for Relevant Client References

- **Vetting:** Ask for client references that have requirements similar to your own company's.
- **Details:** Determine if the LSP has demonstrated success with the specific content (e.g., legal documents, marketing copy, software strings) and languages you need. Don't be afraid to ask for a direct reference contact.

### 4. Assess Financial Stability and Reputation

- **Long-Term View:** If you plan to work with an agency long-term, verify they are financially secure and profitable (check their website or industry rankings like Nimdzi). Also, verify their reputation among freelancers for making timely payments.

### 5. Consider Global Office Locations

- **Time Zone Coverage:** Choose an LSP with offices in both your local market and your target market. This ensures direct contact with translation managers during shared business hours, preventing delays in key project communications.

### 6. Evaluate Technical Capabilities and Integrations

- **Modern Workflows:** Translations require robust technical solutions beyond simple file transfers.
- **Capability:** Choose an agency that has the technical skills to seamlessly integrate their workflow management platform (via **API**) with your company’s systems (like a CMS or a tool like Lang Q). This is essential for nonstandard or complex requirements that off-the-shelf products cannot meet.
